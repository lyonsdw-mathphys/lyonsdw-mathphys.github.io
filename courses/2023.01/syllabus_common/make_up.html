Makeup Assignments and Extra Credit

A makeup quiz will be given during the final exam period for anyone who has been excused from three or more quizzes before 4/7, or who has been excused from 1 or more quizzes after 4/7 (see the section "Attendance" regarding excused absences). The makeup quiz will be up to twice as long as quizzes given before final exam week.

A makeup comprehensive final exam will be given during the final exam period for anyone who has been excused from five or more quizzes before final exam week. The makeup comprehensive final exam covers all of the semester material. The makeup comprehensive final exam will count the same as the total of the excused quizzes.

Format and instructions for a makeup quiz or makeup comprehensive final exam will be announced in advance.

There are no makeup pre-class assignments. There are no extra credit assignments.

Spring 2022 Quiz Procedures

updated: 1/10/2022

Allowed resources

  • No consultation with any other person is allowed. The work you submit must be your own.
  • No textbooks, no notes, no internet sources are allowed.
  • No sound playing devices are allowed during the quiz.
  • Calculator
    • MAS 170: a calculator is required for quizzes. Graphing or business calculators are allowed, but not required. You may not use a calculator on your phone.
    • MAS 162: no calculator is allowed.

Instructions

  • Quizzes begin promptly at the start of class. Be in the classroom (face-to-face participants) or on the class Zoom meeting (for online participants) with video on and with sound off during the quiz.
  • Write your solutions on your own sheet(s) of paper.
  • Copy the answer boxes on the problem sheet to your answer sheet and fill in your answers. You do not need to copy the statements of the problems onto your answer sheet.
  • Show supporting work for each problem that demonstrates how you found your answers.
  • Final answers with no supporting work shown receive no credit!
  • For problems labeled “DISCUSSION”, write your solution using one or more complete sentences. Express the main idea of your solution and use appropriate vocabulary from the text to demonstrate your understanding.
  • When you are finished writing your solutions, use your phone to scan your answer sheet(s) to a single pdf file and then upload to Canvas.
  • Submit only your answer sheet. Your copy of the problem sheet is yours to keep.

Rules

  • During the quiz, do not wear a brimmed hat or a hood or anything that covers your eyes; you may wear a brimless ski hat or you may keep wearing a ball cap if you turn the brim to the back.
  • Place your phone in plain view on your desk (for in-class, face-to-face students) or somewhere in plain view in your Zoom panel (for online students) during the quiz. Do not pick up your phone until you are scanning and submitting your quiz.
  • If you finish and submit before the time limit, you may leave the classroom or the Zoom view for a short break. Leave your phone in plain view on your desk or in your Zoom panel while you are away.

Honor Pledge

  • By submitting your work, you attest to the following statement.
    • "I have not given or received any unauthorized help on this exam. This work is my own."

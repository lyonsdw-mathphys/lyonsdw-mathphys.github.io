Makeup Assignments and Extra Credit

A makeup quiz will be given during the final exam period for anyone who has missed two or more quizzes before the last day of class, or who has missed 1 or more of the last two quizzes before the last day of class. The length of the makeup quiz may vary, depending on how many have been missed.

Format and instructions for a makeup quiz will be announced in advance.

There are no makeup graded exercises assignments. There are no extra credit assignments.

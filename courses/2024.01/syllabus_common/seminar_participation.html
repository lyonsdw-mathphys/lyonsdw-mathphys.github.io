Daily Participation

Daily participation consists of presenting solutions to problems and participating in discussion of presentations made by other students and the instructor. Presentations will be graded for effort and completeness, but not for correctness.

Daily presentations Grading Rubric
Category Description
Complete Presentations are fully prepared in a way that shows a good faith effort to thoroughly complete the assignment.
Partially complete A presentation that should have been thoroughly prepared is not fully complete or shows little effort.
No credit Presentations are not prepared.

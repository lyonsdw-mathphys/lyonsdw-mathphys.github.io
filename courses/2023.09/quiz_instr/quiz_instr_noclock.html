Quiz Instructions Fall 2023

Instructions and rules are subject to change, at the discretion of the instructor.

Instructions

  • Show supporting work!
  • Final answers with no supporting work receive no credit!
  • For “DISCUSSION” problems, use one or more complete sentences:
    • express the main idea
    • use appropriate vocabulary
  • Scan and upload your finished quiz. Exception: No scanning and no Canvas upload for students taking the quiz at CAR.

Rules

  • No books, no notes, no computers. Phones allowed for scanning only after finishing.
  • No earbuds, no air pods, no headphones.
  • No hats with brims.
  • Calculator
    • MAS 113: no calculator is allowed
    • MAS 170: a calculator is allowed (but no phone calculator)

Honor Pledge

  • Check and initial the Pledge box at the top of the quiz to make the following statement:
    • "I have not given or received any unauthorized help on this quiz. This work is my own."

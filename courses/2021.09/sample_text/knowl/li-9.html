Item c.
This sequence cannot be linear or exponential. If the sequence were linear, then \(d\) would have to be \(6-2=4\text{,}\) but then the third term would have to be \(6+4=10\text{.}\) If the sequence were exponential, then \(r\) would have to be \(6/2=3\text{,}\) but then the fourth term would have to be \(18\cdot 3=54\text{.}\)

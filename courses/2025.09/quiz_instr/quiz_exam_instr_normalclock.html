Quiz/Exam Instructions Fall 2025

Instructions and rules are subject to change, at the discretion of the instructor.

Instructions

  • Show supporting work!
  • Final answers with no supporting work receive no credit!
  • For "DISCUSSION" problems, use one or more complete sentences:
    • Express the main idea
    • Use appropriate vocabulary
  • Scan and upload your finished quiz/exam
  • CHECK your upload; log into Canvas and view your uploaded file

Rules

  • No consultation with any other person is allowed
  • NO calculator or any other electronic devices
  • No phones, except to scan your and upload your work
  • No books, no notes
  • No earbuds, no air pods, no headphones
  • No hats with brims; no hoods over the eyes

Honor Pledge

  • Check and initial the Pledge box at the top of the quiz to make the following statement:
    • "I have not given or received any unauthorized help on this quiz/exam. This work is my own."

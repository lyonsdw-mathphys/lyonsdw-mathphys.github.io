Makeup Assignments and Extra Credit

A makeup quiz will be given during the final exam period for quizzes that were missed during excused absence(s). The length of the makeup quiz may vary, depending on how many have been missed.

Format and instructions for a makeup quiz will be announced in advance.

There is no makeup quiz for quizzes that were missed during unexcused absence(s). There are no makeups for graded exercises. There are no extra credit assignments.

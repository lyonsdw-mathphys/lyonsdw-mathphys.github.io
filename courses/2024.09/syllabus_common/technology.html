Required Materials and Technology

In addition to the required course text(s) listed above, the following materials and technology are required.
  • PDF scanner app for your phone (or other device) for scanning your work on one or more pages into a single pdf file (scan-to-pdf capability is a built-in feature of Android and Apple phones; there are also many free apps)
  • Canvas app for your phone (or other device) for submitting your work
  • (for MAS 170 only) Scientific calculator (business or graphing calculators calculators are allowed, but not required)
